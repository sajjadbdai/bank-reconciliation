💡 How Reconciliation Works
✔️ Bank Debit = Money out of bank = Credit in your ledger
✔️ Bank Credit = Money into bank = Debit in your ledger
✔️ Cheque matching by cheque number first
✔️ Timing differences detected (outstanding cheques, bank charges)
✔️ Split transactions matched (100 + 0.110 bank charges)
✔️ Explanation & clarification for every mismatch

⚙️ Matching Settings
Max days apart to consider two transactions as timing-related (e.g., outstanding cheques, bank processing)
Max days between a bank deposit and the individual ledger entries it groups (e.g., several days of cash receipts deposited together)
Amounts will be displayed in BHD with 3 decimal places
🔍 Matching Priority
1st 🏷️ Cheque Number Match — Matches by CHQ# + amount (highest confidence)
2nd 📅 Exact Date + Amount — Same amount, within 1 day
3rd ⏱️ Near-Date Match — Same amount, within tolerance days (timing differences)
4th ✂️ Split Transaction Match — One entry = sum of multiple entries (e.g., grouped cash deposits, or 100 + 0.110 bank charges)
5th ❌ Unmatched — Remaining items flagged for manual review
